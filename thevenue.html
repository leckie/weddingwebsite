Getting there

Use the postcode for your sat nav or use the Google Map below to find your way there. There is car parking at the venue for those who need it.

Kinkell is situated on the coast two miles south of St Andrews on the A917 towards Crail. Coming from St Andrews, the driveway is in the first line of trees on the left.

The farm runs down to the sea and has spectacular views of the coastline to the north and west towards St Andrews. Fingers crossed for sun so we can enjoy them on the day!

The entrance is marked with a sign at either side for 'fyfeoffroad.com and Kinkell Byre', there is also a sign for Kinkell Farm. Turn in here and follow the road until you arrive at the parking area.

The nearest rail station is Leuchars (approximately 20 minutes drive). Buses are available from here to St Andrews.

Dundee is a 30 minute drive from the house, and Edinburgh about an hour and a quarter.
