Some helpful info

We've collated a wee list of places to stay, taxi numbers and some general info.

Hopefully this page gives a wee helping hand with organising your trip to our wedding.

We've included some taxi numbers and a few lovely hotels that you might want to stay in. There's plenty more available though and Tripadvisor is a great place to look.

One thing we would say is please try bring cash along on the day as we're not sure the bar will accept cards!

Local taxi numbers!

  • Golf City Taxis 01334 477788
  • St Andrews Taxis 01334 477272
  • H M Taxis 01334 474700
  • G&A Taxis 01334 871030

  • The Fairmont

    1 mile from venue

    A beautiful upscale resort located close to the Castle Course with 2 championship golf courses, sea view suites, luxury spa and onsite fine dining.

  • Rufflets

    4 miles from venue

    A graceful turreted mansion just outside St. Andrews with plush guest rooms and suites which also provides some tasty seasonal Scottish fine dining.

  • The Ardgowan

    2.6 miles from venue

    Located within the heart of St. Andrews the Ardgowan features simple rooms with tartan motifs in a 19th-century hotel with a steakhouse restaurant.

  • Hotel Du Vin

    2.6 miles from venue

    A lovely refurbished hotel located within St. Andrews with plenty of nooks and crannies. There's no shortage of comfortable rooms and tasy restaurants.
